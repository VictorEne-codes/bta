MEET
THOMAS AREMU

And I will give you pastors according to mine heart,
which shall feed you with knowledge and
understanding.
Jer. 3 : 15

Born on March 18, 1957, in Oyo Town, Oyo State, Bishop Thomas Olayiwola Aremu grew up with values that would define his extraordinary journey, hard work, faith, and an unwavering commitment to his divine calling. As a young man, Thomas pursued a career in accounting, demonstrating remarkable skill and diligence. By 1984, at just 27, he became a Chartered Accountant. A significant achievement. But beyond professional success, what set him apart was his unshakable faith. He believed God was guiding his steps, and this trust propelled his rapid rise in the civil service. Within just 18 months, he advanced from Grade Level-8 to Grade Level-12, gaining recognition for both his competence and integrity. In 1986, a life-changing moment occurred when Bishop David Oyedepo visited Thomas’s home in Sokoto. At the time, Thomas and his wife were believing God for a child. During the visit, Bishop Oyedepo prophetically declared that their waiting was over. That very month, his wife conceived their first daughter, Faith, an undeniable testament to God’s power. Three years later, in 1989, another prophetic word came during a pastors’ gathering. Bishop Oyedepo lifted Thomas’s wife’s hands and declared that she would conceive twins. True to the declaration, the couple welcomed a boy and a girl that same year.

Today, Bishop Thomas Aremu is blessed with four children, in accordance with Psalm 112, and they are excelling in their respective fields: Dr. Faith – A clinical psychologist, Dr. Love – A medical doctor and physiotherapist, Engineer Thomas – A mechanical engineer working in the mining sector in Australia, and Emmanuel – A realtor.

Thomas Aremu didn’t just experience divine interventions in his family life; he also saw God’s hand in his material blessings. While working on the Dominion Cathedral in Kaduna in 1989, Bishop Oyedepo declared that all involved in the project would build their own houses. By 1990, Thomas had completed his first home, another proof of God’s provision. Then came the pivotal moment of surrender. In 1994, he answered the call to full-time ministry. That May, Bishop Oyedepo confirmed God’s instruction to plant a new church in Ibadan. On June 5, 1994, with just 23 people in attendance,

the Ibadan Church was born. What followed was nothing short of a revival. By 1998, membership had surged past 10,000, and the impact extended beyond Ibadan into neighboring states. Thomas Aremu’s ministry journey took him to several cities, each time leaving behind a trail of unprecedented growth: Ilorin (1998–2004): Membership grew from 4,000 to 17,000. Kaduna (2004–2007): The congregation expanded from 20,000 to 27,000. In 2008, he embraced an international mandate, serving as Regional Overseer for South Africa and ten other countries. Under his leadership in Johannesburg, the church doubled in size within a year, and 12 new churches were planted across South Africa. His impact extended even further when he was appointed Vice President, Africa, and later Vice President of Foreign Missions,

overseeing global church expansion. Bishop Thomas Aremu’s global assignment continued as he was appointed Vice President in charge of the Mission Inspectorate. In this role, he was tasked with overseeing mission inspections across all nations where the church is represented. His responsibilities included assessing global church operations and reporting key findings, highlighting areas that required attention and corrective measures directly to the President and Apostle over the Commission. The pinnacle of

his global leadership came when he was appointed as a member of the Global Board of Trustees, a role that placed him at the heart of the Commission’s strategic decision-making and governance. From his humble beginnings in Oyo Town to his global influence as a spiritual leader, Bishop Thomas Olayiwola Aremu’s life is a testament to grace, obedience, and unwavering faith in God’s call. His journey is not just a story of personal success but a blueprint of divine purpose. A life dedicated to transforming lives, building the kingdom, and fulfilling destiny.

Bishop Thomas Aremu is an anointed man of God sent to bring God’s word to our generation.
