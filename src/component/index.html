Born on March 18, 1957, in Oyo Town, Oyo State, Bishop Thomas Olayiwola Aremu grew up with values that would define his extraordinary journey, hard work, faith, and an unwavering commitment to his divine calling. As a young man, Thomas pursued a career in accounting, demonstrating remarkable skill and diligence. By 1984, at just 27, he became a Chartered Accountant. A significant achievement. But beyond professional success, what set him apart was his unshakable faith.
