ACADEMIC SPONSORSHIPS

Education is a powerful tool for transformation, but for many, financial limitations stand in the way of their dreams. Through Covenant Liberality, we take on the divine mandate to lift others by providing academic sponsorships to deserving students. Our commitment to education is rooted in the understanding that knowledge empowers individuals to fulfill their God-given destinies. By investing in students, we are not just funding their education, we are shaping the future, raising kingdom giants, and advancing the work of God on earth. As Proverbs 22:9 declares, "He that hath a bountiful eye shall be blessed; for he giveth of his bread to the poor." Every seed sown into the life of a student yields a harvest of generational impact.

WIDOWS OUTREACH

Pure and undefiled religion before God is caring for those in need (James 1:27). At the heart of Covenant Liberality is our commitment to uplifting widows, women who have suffered loss yet remain pillars of strength in their families and communities. Through our Widows Outreach, we extend the love of Christ by providing financial support, empowerment programs, and spiritual encouragement. We believe that no widow should be left without help, and as a church, we stand as a family to ensure they experience God’s provision and care. As Psalm 68:5 reminds us, “A father of the fatherless, and a judge of the widows, is God in his holy habitation.” By giving to and supporting widows, we align with God's heart and unlock the blessings of divine provision and favor.

ORPHANAGES

God is a Father to the fatherless, and as His hands and feet on earth, we are called to care for orphans and vulnerable children (Psalm 68:5). Through our orphanage outreach, we extend love, provision, and support to children who have been left without parental care, ensuring they experience the warmth of a family and the security of a future filled with hope. Covenant Liberality enables us to meet their needs. Providing food, shelter, education, and spiritual guidance. Beyond meeting physical needs, we introduce them to the love of Christ, assuring them that they are not forgotten but deeply cherished by God and His people.

MOTHERLESS BABIES HOMES

Every child deserves love, care, and a bright future. Sadly, many infants are left without the nurturing embrace of a mother. Through Covenant Liberality, we extend God’s love to motherless babies, ensuring they receive the care, support, and warmth they need to thrive. Our outreach to motherless babies' homes provides essential supplies such as food, clothing, medical care, and shelter. But beyond physical needs, we offer something even greater hope. We stand as a family to these little ones, reminding them that they are not forgotten, but deeply loved by God and His people.

ELDERLY HOMES

The elderly are treasures of wisdom, experience, and faith. Vessels who have labored in life’s journey and deserve honor, care, and dignity in their later years. Through Covenant Liberality, we extend the love of Christ to the elderly in homes, ensuring they are not forgotten but cherished and supported. Our outreach provides essential supplies, medical care, companionship, and spiritual encouragement. We recognize that loneliness and neglect are some of the greatest challenges the elderly face, and through our acts of love, we restore joy, dignity, and a sense of belonging to them.

MEDICAL OUTREACHES

Good health is a gift from God, and as stewards of His love, we are called to extend healing and care to those in need. Through Covenant Liberality, our Medical Outreach provides essential healthcare services to the less privileged, ensuring that no one is left to suffer due to a lack of medical attention. From free medical checkups and treatments to health education and support, we bring both physical healing and the hope of Christ to individuals and communities. Just as Jesus went about healing the sick (Matthew 4:24), we follow His example by ministering to the body, soul, and spirit of those in need.
